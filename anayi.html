Anayirankal, Munnar

Located in the midst of a verdant carpet of tea plantations, Anayirankal is an ideal picnic spot for travellers from around the world.
The Anayirankal Dam is flanked by the Tata Tea Plantation and a thick cover of evergreen forests. Anayirankal is located 22 km from Munnar,
the beloved hill station of Kerala. A boat ride on the splendid reservoir is an unforgettable experience that no visitor should miss.
