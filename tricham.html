Trichambaram Temple

A sacred place of the Vaishnava cult that predates the 10th century, with Lord Krishna being the primary deity,
the Trichambaram Temple in Kannur is among the most historic temples in the district.
It is here that visitors get to view Lord Krishna in Raudra (ferocious posture) after the Kamsavadham - killing of the demon king Kamsan.
